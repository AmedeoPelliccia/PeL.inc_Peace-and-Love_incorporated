## 1. Purpose

**Union Realities (UR)** is the convergence framework that unifies every reality modality into a single, coherent operational layer within the GAIA-QAO Universal Technology Classification System (UTCS).

Where Extended Reality (XR) aggregates Virtual, Augmented, and Mixed realities side by side, **UR fuses them** — together with Physical Reality (PR), Diminished Reality (DR), and Quantum Reality (QR) — into one seamless continuum governed by the Transgentle axioms and the QASHT protocol.

```
UR = ⋃ Rᵢ   where Rᵢ ∈ { PR, VR, AR, MR, DR, XR, QR }
```

UR is not a new modality added to the list — it is the **set-theoretic union** of all modalities, with coherent state synchronization across boundaries.
