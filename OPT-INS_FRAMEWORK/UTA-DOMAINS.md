## Overview

The **Universal Technology Architecture (UTA)** extends the OPT-INS 6-axis topology
to a **1 000-chapter taxonomy** across **10 domain groups** (G1–G10).  
Each domain group contains 100 chapters organised into 10 decade blocks,
and every decade block maps to exactly one OPT-INS axis (O / P / T / I / N / S).
