# QCSAA 920-929 · Section 02 · Subsection 10 — Quantum networks

## 1. Purpose

Overview entry-point for the *Quantum networks* subsection within the `920-929` code range (Section `02` — *Redes y Comunicaciones Cuánticas*) of the **QCSAA** architecture band (*Quantum Computing & Sentient Agency Architecture*, master range `900–999`).

This subsubject (`00 Overview`) introduces the QCSAA 920-929.10.00 slice and links it to the controlled Q+ATLANTIDE baseline[^baseline] and to the applicable industry standards listed in §4.

## 2. Scope

- Covers the *Quantum networks* slice of the parent code range `920-929`.
- Inherits Q-Division authority and ORB support from the parent row in [`../../README.md` §3](../../README.md#3-architecture-table)[^archtable].
- Subsequent subsubjects (`01`–`99`) under this subsection extend this Overview with detailed data modules per S1000D[^s1000d].
