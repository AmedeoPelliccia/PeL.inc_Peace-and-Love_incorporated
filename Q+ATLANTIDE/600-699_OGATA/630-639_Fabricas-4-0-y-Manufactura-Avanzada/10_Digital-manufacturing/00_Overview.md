# OGATA 630-639 · Section 03 · Subsection 10 — Digital manufacturing

## 1. Purpose

Overview entry-point for the *Digital manufacturing* subsection within the `630-639` code range (Section `03` — *Fábricas 4.0 y Manufactura Avanzada*) of the **OGATA** architecture band (*On-Ground Automation Technology Architecture*, master range `600–699`).

This subsubject (`00 Overview`) introduces the OGATA 630-639.10.00 slice and links it to the controlled Q+ATLANTIDE baseline[^baseline] and to the applicable industry standards listed in §4.

## 2. Scope

- Covers the *Digital manufacturing* slice of the parent code range `630-639`.
- Inherits Q-Division authority and ORB support from the parent row in [`../../README.md` §3](../../README.md#3-architecture-table)[^archtable].
- Subsequent subsubjects (`01`–`99`) under this subsection extend this Overview with detailed data modules per S1000D[^s1000d].
