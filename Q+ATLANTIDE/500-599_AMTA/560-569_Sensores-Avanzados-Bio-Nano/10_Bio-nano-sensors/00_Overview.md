# AMTA 560-569 · Section 06 · Subsection 10 — Bio/nano sensors

## 1. Purpose

Overview entry-point for the *Bio/nano sensors* subsection within the `560-569` code range (Section `06` — *Sensores Avanzados Bio/Nano*) of the **AMTA** architecture band (*Advanced Material, Bio & Nanotechnology Architecture*, master range `500–599`).

This subsubject (`00 Overview`) introduces the AMTA 560-569.10.00 slice and links it to the controlled Q+ATLANTIDE baseline[^baseline] and to the applicable industry standards listed in §4.

## 2. Scope

- Covers the *Bio/nano sensors* slice of the parent code range `560-569`.
- Inherits Q-Division authority and ORB support from the parent row in [`../../README.md` §3](../../README.md#3-architecture-table)[^archtable].
- Subsequent subsubjects (`01`–`99`) under this subsection extend this Overview with detailed data modules per S1000D[^s1000d].
