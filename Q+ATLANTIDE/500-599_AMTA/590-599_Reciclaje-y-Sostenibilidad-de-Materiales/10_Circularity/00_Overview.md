# AMTA 590-599 · Section 09 · Subsection 10 — Circularity

## 1. Purpose

Overview entry-point for the *Circularity* subsection within the `590-599` code range (Section `09` — *Reciclaje y Sostenibilidad de Materiales*) of the **AMTA** architecture band (*Advanced Material, Bio & Nanotechnology Architecture*, master range `500–599`).

This subsubject (`00 Overview`) introduces the AMTA 590-599.10.00 slice and links it to the controlled Q+ATLANTIDE baseline[^baseline] and to the applicable industry standards listed in §4.

## 2. Scope

- Covers the *Circularity* slice of the parent code range `590-599`.
- Inherits Q-Division authority and ORB support from the parent row in [`../../README.md` §3](../../README.md#3-architecture-table)[^archtable].
- Subsequent subsubjects (`01`–`99`) under this subsection extend this Overview with detailed data modules per S1000D[^s1000d].
