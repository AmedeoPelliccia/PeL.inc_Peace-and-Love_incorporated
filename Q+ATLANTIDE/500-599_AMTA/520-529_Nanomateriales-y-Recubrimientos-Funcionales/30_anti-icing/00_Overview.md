# AMTA 520-529 · Section 02 · Subsection 30 — anti-icing

## 1. Purpose

Overview entry-point for the *anti-icing* subsection within the `520-529` code range (Section `02` — *Nanomateriales y Recubrimientos Funcionales*) of the **AMTA** architecture band (*Advanced Material, Bio & Nanotechnology Architecture*, master range `500–599`).

This subsubject (`00 Overview`) introduces the AMTA 520-529.30.00 slice and links it to the controlled Q+ATLANTIDE baseline[^baseline] and to the applicable industry standards listed in §4.

## 2. Scope

- Covers the *anti-icing* slice of the parent code range `520-529`.
- Inherits Q-Division authority and ORB support from the parent row in [`../../README.md` §3](../../README.md#3-architecture-table)[^archtable].
- Subsequent subsubjects (`01`–`99`) under this subsection extend this Overview with detailed data modules per S1000D[^s1000d].
